About us

LAWA has been operating on the market since 1990. From the beginning of our work, we have been providing top-shelf fire fighting and personal protection equipment. We have extensive experience in acquiring and distributing specialized footwear, overalls and firefighting helmets. LAWA's offer also includes the highest quality disinfectants and protective agents. These are excellent products that prove necessary in the daily fight against the COVID-19 virus. They will ensure the safety of you and your loved ones. Disinfectants and protective clothing will keep your business running safely and continuously.

Thanks to the foreign contacts developed over the years, LAWA also offers equipment and military equipment obtained from the US Army Inviolable Stocks (ZN). Cooperation with contractors from Italy and Georgia allows us to provide the highest quality goods at attractive prices.

In recognition of the professionalism and excellent quality of the services provided, LAWA is the winner of the "Firma na medal" competition. We are also a member of the "Polish-Georgian Chamber of Commerce"
